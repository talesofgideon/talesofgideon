Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 23, 2026

1. Clear Privacy Philosophy

Thomas Gideon ("we," "us," or "our") deeply respects your digital privacy. This website is designed to be as minimal and non-intrusive as possible. We do not maintain or run any database hosting private individual profiles, we do not run tracking cookies to gather browser histories, and we do not maintain email marketing directories on this host.

2. Secure Transactions via Paddle

All ecommerce transactions, payments, financial verification, invoice distributions, and subsequent secure file delivery are processed, secured, and fulfilled by Paddle, our merchant of record. Any payment details, billing coordinates, or contact information required to finalize your EPUB download are handled entirely within Paddle's secure external systems in accordance with their strict privacy standards.

3. No Tracking Cookies

This website does not employ analytical, advertising, or tracking cookies to monitor your browser habits. We utilize standard browser localStorage purely to remember your chosen user preference (specifically, your preference for dark or light appearance setting) so that the pages display beautifully each time you visit.

4. External Links

Our webpages may link to external networks (such as official social media book channels). Please note that once you navigate off this site, those services operate under their own independent terms of use and data policies.

5. Direct Enquiries

If you reach out to us with questions about our EPUB books or music soundtracks, we only use your message content to answer your query. Your inquiry details are never repurposed, stored in user accounts, or shared with external third parties.
