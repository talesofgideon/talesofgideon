COMING SOON

About the Artist

I can't exactly remember when the strange dreams started. I remember reading somewhere to record these dreams, instead of simply shaking them off. Exodus, is the first novel exploring these dreams, its part of a trilogy of stories and music.

It begins while Conner is working as a reporter of the strange, investigating strange incidents across America. Along his journey he meets a stranger who changes his life forever. He no longer reports on the strange, he becomes strange.

Like when someone has a tune in their head and passes it on to you, I'm passing my dreams on. I hope you enjoy this debut novel.
