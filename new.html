Exodus is a science fiction novel that follows Conner Gideon, son of an American Airforce officer, who, probably because of his fathers work, becomes interested at an early age in unidentified flying objects (UFO's) and unexplainable incidents.

His journey, unlike his father’s follows a path that would lead him to reporting strange incidences across America including UFO's, interviewing hundreds of witnesses and asking sometime the unanswerable question.

With help from his computer genius friend AJ he uses technology to try and answers this question, which ultimately leads to Conner questioning his own reality.

If you were Conner Gideon, what would you do, when your world, destiny, and perception of reality are altered, forever.
